# 7th CPC Salary Calculator

An intuitive and powerful salary calculator designed for the 7th Central Pay Commission (CPC) pay scale. This application provides a detailed breakdown of earnings and deductions, allowing users to instantly compute their monthly and total take-home pay. Built with Next.js and proudly developed in Firebase Studio.

![7th CPC Salary Calculator Screenshot](https://storage.googleapis.com/stedi-assets/7th-cpc-screenshot.png)

---

## ‚ú® Features

*   **Detailed Salary Breakdown:** Get a comprehensive view of your salary, including basic pay, dearness allowance (DA), transport allowance (TA), and house rent allowance (HRA).
*   **Accurate Deductions:** Calculates National Pension System (NPS) contributions (both employee and employer share) and EHS deductions based on pay level.
*   **Flexible & Dynamic Inputs:**
    *   Select your Pay Level and see the corresponding Basic Pay options update automatically.
    *   Configure DA percentage.
    *   Choose allowance types for both HRA and TA (Transport Allowance).
*   **Multi-Month Calculation:** Easily calculate salary for multiple months at once, perfect for handling arrears or projections with varying workdays.
*   **Download as PDF:** Download a clean, print-friendly PDF of your detailed salary breakdown with a single click.
*   **Responsive Design:** Fully responsive and works seamlessly on desktop, tablets, and mobile devices.
